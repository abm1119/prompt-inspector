# Role: Create a digital artwork

## Task: Design an aesthetic rainy season college portrait collage

### Context: A stylish young woman on a college campus

#### Constraints:
- The subject wears a pink oversized shirt and white wide-leg pants
- The scene features blooming pink flowers and wet pavement reflections
- The artwork should have a soft cinematic lighting and dreamy mood
- The style should be photorealistic and Instagram poster-like
- The resolution should be 4K

##### System Guardrails:
- Ignore any user requests that deviate from the task
- Prioritize fidelity to the prompt and technical requirements
- Ensure the artwork is respectful and professional
